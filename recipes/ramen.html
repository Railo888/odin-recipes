Ramen

Description

This dish is basically quality noodles with rich soup in a beautiful bowl.

Ingredients

  • fish cake
  • ramen noodles
  • soup
  • boiled eggs
  • Seasoned bamboo shoots
  • roasted pork

Steps

  1. boiling hot water with a pot
  2. putting noodles in it
  3. waiting enough time to soften noodles
  4. draining hot water from noodles
  5. Putting noodles, fish cake, boiled eggs, Seasoned bamboo shoots, and roasted pork in a bowl you've already prepared
  6. filling a bowl with soup to the top
